Amsterdam Walking Tour

See the best of Amsterdam with the fun Amsterdam Walking Tour! This fascinating walking tour through the city will show you the most interesting things in Amsterdam - both the famous and the normally overlooked. The tour has a duration of approximately two and a half hours during which you will be accompanied by an expert tour guide who will give you interesting information along the way.

Some of the highlights include the historical Dam Square, the world-famous Red Light District, and the old beguinage Begijnhof. You will also have a chance to sample some of the Netherlands’ most traditional delicacies, including waffles and jenever. The Walking Tour is the perfect way to make the most out of your time in Amsterdam.

Discover the best things to do in Amsterdam

Whether you’re staying in town for a couple of days or just passing through, the Amsterdam Walking Tour will show you the top attractions and things to do in Amsterdam. The enthusiastic guide will take you to the key points of interest and provide you with fascinating information (in English and in Spanish) about the sights along the way. You will learn how the city developed over time, how the historic canal district came to be and have a chance to walk through the Red Light District.

Visit places Amsterdam is famous for and much more! The tour also includes a tasting of the following traditional Dutch delicacies: stroopwafels (syrup waffles), haring (pickled herring) and jenever (Dutch gin). The Amsterdam Walking Tour is one of the most complete and fun things to do in the city!
